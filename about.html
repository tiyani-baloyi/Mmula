Welcome to Mmula Group

More About Us

Mmula Group is a 100% Black, South African owned and registered enterprise listed on the CIPC database, registration number 2019/629682/07. The company is based in Vryburg, North West Province. The company is solely owned by Mr. Puso Monametsi who has been operating in the recycling sector for more than 6 years

Mmula Group aims to expand its existing waste sorting and bailing plant and by doing so broaden the economic empowerment of preciously disadvantaged individuals and provides skills development and environmental awareness within the local community. The business buys recyclables from waste collectors and collects waste from the Dr Ruth Segomotsi Mompati District Municipality and salvage waste from the nearest landfill site.
